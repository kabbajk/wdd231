Website Plan

Fresh & Clean Dry Cleaners

Site Name

Fresh & Clean Dry Cleaners

This name was selected because it clearly communicates the service we provide (dry cleaning) and the quality customers can expect (fresh and clean results). The name is simple, memorable, and conveys trustworthiness.

Optional domain availability: freshandcleandry.com (available for $12.99/year)

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about our dry cleaning services, attract new customers, and offer convenient online features for existing customers.

Specifically, the site will:

  • Showcase our services and pricing
  • Allow customers to schedule pickups and deliveries
  • Provide store locations and hours
  • Offer care tips for different fabrics
  • Display special offers and loyalty programs
  • Enable contact with customer service

Scenarios

These are questions that potential visitors might ask:

  • What are your hours of operation and where are you located?
  • Do you offer pickup and delivery services?
  • How much does it cost to dry clean a suit/wedding dress/coat?
  • What's your turnaround time for dry cleaning?
  • Do you offer any discounts or loyalty programs?
  • How do I remove a specific stain from my clothing?

Color Schema

The color palette for the website reflects cleanliness and professionalism while maintaining a fresh, approachable feel:

Primary (#005b96)

Used for headers, navigation, and primary buttons - conveys trust and professionalism

Secondary (#f7b733)

Used for call-to-action buttons and highlights - adds energy and draws attention

Accent (#4abdac)

Used for secondary buttons and interactive elements - represents freshness and cleanliness

Background (#f5f5f5)

Used as the main background color - creates a clean, light appearance

Typography

The following fonts will be used:

Montserrat

Used for all headings (H1, H2, H3, etc.) - clean, modern, and professional

Bold weight for important headings and calls to action

Roboto

Used for body text and paragraphs - highly readable and pairs well with Montserrat
